who am I?

I was born in Izmir in the late 1980s and lived here until I was 7 years old, and then I moved to Cyprus with my family.From that day until today, my whole life has been spent in Cyprus. I have lived my life with graphic design and music until today, but at the age of 33 I decided to become a software developer. Everything that is seen is a part of this journey.

drummer

I started playing drums at the age of 16 out of pure interest in pots and buckets at home. I have brought my level to a certain point with the courses I have followed online over time and I have been taking the stage actively for 14 years. After performing with various musicians in Cyprus, I've been playing with Dilara Ferit for the last 4 years.

graphic designer

After completing my graphic design education that I started in 2005 in 2010, I took a position as a Graphic Designer in various companies in Izmir and Cyprus. I have been working at Major Music Center for the last 10 years. I want to combine my profession with web design and improve myself.
